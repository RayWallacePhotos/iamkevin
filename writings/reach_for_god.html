Reach For God

by Kevin J. McCarthy


Like your quick approach for the apple in the
Strongly-branched tree in the field behind the house -
Reach for God.


Outstretched gloved right hand while rock climbing -
Like the small crevice inches away from your
Reach for God.


Like the dying elderly man raising his arm very
Slowly one last time towards his son as he leaves -
Reach for God.
